Get Started with BrainDance

Modular Experiment Design

Design complex neural stimulation experiments with ease. BrainDance's phase-based system allows you to combine recording, stimulation, and analysis modules for sophisticated protocols.

Real-time Control

Achieve precise control over stimulation parameters and timing. Interact with micro electrode arrays in real-time, adjusting your experiment on the fly based on neural responses.

Integrated Analysis Tools

From raw data processing to advanced statistical methods, BrainDance provides built-in tools for visualizing and analyzing your experimental results, streamlining your research workflow.
